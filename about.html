About Us

About Shiv Shakti Bal Academy

Shiv Shakti Bal Academy, established in 2024, is a premier English medium school in Mirchaiya, Siraha. Our institution stands as a beacon of quality education, combining traditional values with modern teaching methodologies. Located in Chandra Ayodhyapur, we serve communities from Mirchaiya, Choharwa, and Golbazar, making us one of the most accessible and prestigious educational institutions in the region.
